Dial 999

Your answers suggest you need
to dial 999 immediately and ask
for an ambulance.

What to do in the meantime...

  • If the patient is cold, cover them with a blanket.
  • Don't give an injured person anything to eat or drink.
  • Have someone watch for the ambulance and show the crew how to get to the patient.
  • Stay calm and try to keep the patient calm.
  • Don't move a patient who was injured in an automobile accident or fall, or who was found unconscious.

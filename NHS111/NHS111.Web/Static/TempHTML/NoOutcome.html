Self care : Headache

Call NHS 111 for a further assessment if:

  • your symptoms change or get worse
  • you develop any new symptoms
  • you have any further concerns

It's free from
any landline or mobile phone

 

Headaches are a common problem and will be experienced by most people, either regularly, or at some point in their lives. The pain may be felt in the head, the face or the neck. They can be caused by a number of things including dehydration, tension or eye strain. Headaches that happen repeatedly can include migraines or cluster headaches (headaches that can occur for a short time, several times a day, and over a period of several weeks)
